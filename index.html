Our Story

Yehova Nissy Ministries, founded by Pastor T. Adamu Garu (went to glory), has been doing ministry for 40+ years, coming from poor beginnings depending on God and faith.

Present Pastor T. Daniel has been doing ministry for the last 10 years. Main vision: Spread the Gospel to the World through various ministries.
